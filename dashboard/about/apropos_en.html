About Us

Our team, called DPA Green Tech, is composed of four (04) members, including one woman, from training schools such as: the Sub-Regional Institute of Statistics and Applied Economics (ISSEA) and the National Advanced School of Engineering of Yaoundé (ENSPY).

Our Motivation

Our motivation for participating in the Hackathon 2026 Cameroon is based on our shared desire to innovate and offer concrete technological solutions to our environment's challenges. We are convinced that the combination of data, technological development, and strategic thinking can generate a sustainable impact in Cameroon.

Hackathon IndabaX Cameroon 2026

Our Mission:

  • Predict air pollution indicators from meteorological data
  • Identify climate-related aggravating factors in different zones
  • Design clear and accessible decision-support tools for decision-makers and communities
